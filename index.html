Our Firm

Originally founded in Lagos in 1991 as Tayo Oyetibo & Co., we have since moved from a litigation only practice to become a leading Dispute Resolution and Commercial Law Firm in Nigeria. In 2016 we became TAYO OYETIBO LP (TOLP) after registering as a Limited Partnership under the Laws of Lagos State. In January 2022 TOLP merged with the commercial law firm of Twelve Legal and both firms have since continued practice as one integrated legal practice under the TOLP brand.




The TOLP Difference

We know that, for clients, the ideal law firm is one that not only provides quality legal advice and representation, but also has a complete understanding of the client's business challenges, issues and economics, provides true value, is responsive and stays focused on the client's commercial goals.

Criminal Litigation Practice Unit

Criminal litigations have always been a hallmark of our disputes practice. We act for individual, government and corporate clients in every aspect of criminal law, including criminal investigations and litigations relating to asset tracing claims, corporate fraud, extradition, bribery and corruption, business and regulatory crime, official secrets, intellectual property and miscellaneous offences. We represent clients at all levels of courts, from the High Courts to the Supreme Court. We are regularly instructed to act in some of the most sensitive and high-profile criminal litigations in Nigeria.

Public Law Litigation Practice Unit

Our expertise in public law litigation is wide-ranging and highly regarded. Public law litigations have historically been one of the foundations of TOLP’s practice since our establishment. Our team of lawyers have an exceptional reputation in all areas of public law and we are regularly instructed in high profile and complex judicial reviews and public law considerations in other cases. We draw upon expertise in several different areas, including administrative law, constitutional law, electoral law, environmental law, human rights law, immigration law and international law. This means we can always provide a team of experts for each case and cover a full range of public law matters. We have been involved in several public law litigations of high national and international importance and interest, including post-election litigations challenging elections to the senior public offices like those of the President of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, State Governors, Senators and other legislative offices.

Property and General Litigation Practice Unit

We have one of the most robust property litigation teams in Nigeria and our broad expertise means we can support clients with all their property litigation requirements. We act for property developers, landholding families, governments, property investors, utilities companies, landowners, landlords and tenants concerning all types of property litigations. Such cases include landlord and tenant disputes, right of way and easement disputes, title to land disputes and land transaction fraud disputes. Lawyers in our Property and General Litigation Practice Unit also advise and represent clients in a wide range of general and civil litigation disputes. These include child rights, construction, consumer protection, defamation, employment, family and probate, matrimonial matters, trusts.

Arbitration and Commercial Litigation Practice Unit

Lawyers in our Arbitration and Commercial Litigation Practice Unit typically represent businesspeople, governments, Nigerian and multinational corporates in some of the most important domestic and international commercial disputes. We act in some of the most high-profile and groundbreaking commercial cases in Nigeria. Our commercial litigation lawyers have the depth and expertise to conduct heavyweight litigation in any court in Nigeria. Our work in this area covers a wide range of disputes relating but not limited to banking and finance, companies, debt recovery and insolvency, insurance, intellectual property, international trade, judgment enforcement oil and gas, shipping, tax and transportation. Our arbitration practice is led by leading arbitration practitioners who have significant experience in domestic and international arbitrations arising from diverse industries. Our lawyers have acted in arbitrations under a diverse number of rules, including UNCITRAL, ICC, LCA, SIAC and LMDC.

Company Secretarial Practice Unit

Our company secretarial services are delivered through our wholly owned subsidiary entity, TOLP Corporate Services. Our company secretarial practice is made up of highly skilled lawyers with extensive experience in Nigerian corporate law. We provide innovative company secretarial services to both small and large size corporations that operate businesses in various market sectors. Intellectual Property and Technology Practice Unit

Our intellectual property team provides a full range of commercial, intellectual property law, privacy, sourcing and technology law services for clients. We provide advice and representation to foreign and domestic clients in connection with the identification, protection, exploitation, management and enforcement of their intellectual property rights. We advise our clients as to their legal obligations in terms of registration and renewal of their IP licenses, exploitation and transfer of their rights, assertion of their ownership against infringement and competition, and regulatory compliance with national and international laws. Clients of our technology practice include some of the world’s largest technology companies and we advise on a wide range of technology issues such as the development, use, protection, regulation, transfer, protection and exploitation of new technology and technology products.

Real Estate Practice Unit

We routinely act for governments, landholders, financial institutions, investors, landlords, tenants, dealers and developers in various types of real estate transactions. These include acquisition, investment, development, redevelopment and refinancing. We have great experience in investigating and deducing title, drafting conveyancing documentation, obtaining statutory consent and registering land documentation at the land registries. B&I lawyers are familiar with the different perfection processes that obtain in different parts of the country and are able to close clients' transactions, without geographical limitations. Our real estate lawyers assist clients to protect their interests at every stage of property development, including planning, due diligence, perfection of title, corporate structures and joint development agreements. We also advise both landlords and tenants in relation to both residential and commercial leases.

Regulatory Compliance and Business Advisory Practice Unit

We have, over the years, developed and maintained useful relationships with key regulatory agencies which are significantly advantageous to our clients with respect to regulatory compliance matters generally. We have substantial expertise and experience in providing advice to clients on regulatory compliance matters. We advise local and foreign clients engaged in international transactions who conduct business in Nigeria, either directly or through subsidiaries, on the complexities of Nigerian law and regulations. We work with clients to draft anti-corruption policies and procedures, respond to cross-border information requests and conduct legal and regulatory audits. Our clients typically operate in a diverse range of industries, including oil and gas, construction, infrastructure, fast moving consumer goods and consultancy services. Our lawyers advise on various registrations and compliance matters with governmental agencies such as the Nigerian Investment Promotion Commission, Corporate Affairs Commission, Federal Inland Revenue Service, Standard Organization of Nigeria and Nigerian Upstream Petroleum Regulatory Commission.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Pro Bono Work

At TOLP we always aim to make a difference in everything that we do. Our strong desire to always make a difference means that we are committed to having an impact on our community and the lives of those that live in it. As part of our Corporate Social Responsibility, we provide pro bono legal services to indigent clients in criminal matters. This way, in deserving cases, we are able to provide access to justice for those who may ordinarily be unable to obtain qualitative legal services or remedies to their pressing problems. To assist in achieving these objectives, we are a participant in the Lagos Public Interest Law Partnership, which was established to enhance access to justice for indigent, marginalized and vulnerable people in society.

Law FM 103.9 FM

LAW FM 103.9 is the pioneer Law Radio Station in Nigeria and indeed, Africa. LAW FM was founded by TOLP’s Chief Counsel- Tayo Oyetibo, SAN, and is an affiliate of TOLP. The Station is established to broadcast for knowledge and entertainment. LAW FM 103.9 is significantly dedicated to broadcasting socio-legal issues and content that affect the day-to-day transactions and activities of the people, in a precise, innovative, educative, clear, and entertaining (PIECE) manner. There are many activities and transactions that people engage in, on a daily basis, that have serious legal implications. Therefore, LAW FM’s objective is to help listeners know about the law and their rights, such that they can be guided in taking the initial decisions in their daily transactions and activities that will ultimately put them on the right path. Located in Lekki in Lagos State of Nigeria, LAW FM 103.9 commenced commercial broadcasting in February 2021. Click HERE for more information about LAW FM 103.9.
