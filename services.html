TOLP Corporate Services

TOLP Corporate Services is a wholly owned subsidiary entity of TOLP through which TOLP provides company secretarial and corporate governance services to clients.

Our company secretarial practice is made up of highly skilled lawyers with extensive experience in Nigerian corporate law. We provide innovative company secretarial services to both small and large size corporations that operate businesses in various market sectors.

We arrange board and general meetings for companies and provide comprehensive and articulate minutes of meetings to the clients within a short period of time. Our lawyers are diligent, proficient and well-trained in dealing with all of our clients. The team at TOLP Corporate Services is led by experienced TOLP Partner, Mrs. Abiola Tayo-Oyetibo.

Our full suite of corporate services include:

Company Secretarial Services

- Corporate searches and due diligence. - Provision of company secretarial support to Incorporated Trustees. - Incorporation and establishment of corporate entities in Nigeria. - Coordination of board meetings, board committee meetings and general meetings. - Circulation of board packs, and preparation of the minutes of meetings of the board, board committees and general meetings.  - Maintenance of corporate seal and statutory registers (including minute books, register of members and register of directors and their interests). - Filing of all statutory returns, including annual returns, relevant resolutions, changes in the composition of the board or the shareholding structure, or charges against the company’s assets. - Corporate reorganization, re-registration, and dissolution. 

Citizenship By Investment (Antigua & Barbuda)

We offer full-service for the acquisition of citizenship by investment within a reasonable time frame. Corporate Governance Services

- Board succession planning and advisory. - Director selection and board training.  - Governance audit of an organization’s level of compliance with corporate governance principles and practices. - Board evaluation to assess the effectiveness of the board, board committees and individual directors. - Codification of board and committee charters. - Codification of policies including on anti-discrimination, anti-bribery and corruption, Code of Ethics, Whistle Blowing, Conflict Resolution, etc.

Other Corporate Services

- Notarization of documents.  - Provision of resident directors and registered office address. - Provision of nominee directors and shareholders. - Bank account opening.
