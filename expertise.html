Our Expertise

ARBITRATION

  • Leading a consortium of law firms advising and representing the Federal Government of Nigeria (FGN) in relation to a dispute between the FGN and a gas processing company in respect of an arbitral award worth over $9 Billion arising from a transaction concerning a large gas processing facility in Nigeria.
